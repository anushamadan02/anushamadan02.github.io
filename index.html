Mobile Covid Sanitization Robot
 Anusha Madan; Arati Ganesh; Preethi N; Sahana Bandekar; A.N. Nagashree
2021 International Symposium of Asian Control Association on Intelligent Robotics and Industrial Automation (IRIA), IEEE Explore
Details
  • This paper presents the development of a robotic system called "Covid Bot," designed for sanitizing environments to help control the spread of COVID-19. It includes a detailed design and implementation of both software and hardware components.
  • Technical Specifications: The robot uses MQTT protocol for tele-operation, Raspberry Pi as the controller, and incorporates features like a camera, sensors, and a sanitizing mechanism. It's designed to operate remotely for efficient sanitization.
  • Functionality: The robot can perform sanitization through a spray system and a hand sanitizer dispenser. It's equipped with a camera and sensors for navigation and obstacle avoidance.
  • Results and Potential Improvements: The robot demonstrated effective sanitization capabilities in controlled environments. Future improvements could include autonomous navigation and more sophisticated control systems for the sanitization process.
